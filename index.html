Play Spinterlands like an Idle Game

Tue, Mar 17, 2020 What is Idle games? Idle games, or “games that play themselves”, are games which are all about management of revenue streams. The core idea of these games is that progress can be made without a player’s constant involvement. Player mainly focus on growth decisions. These games are dominating many of the popular flash portals and shooting up the charts on mobile. You earn in-game currency and buy upgrades. After that, you can put the game away and just check it once in a while; your purchased upgrades will earn money for you.
